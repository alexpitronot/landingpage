About

The company was founded in 2020 by a team of engineers and programmers with rich experience. The main activity of the company is software development, providing solution for the integration and management of several unrelated applications and security devices through a single user interface. Based on the rich experience of working in the field launching and integrating such systems and taking into consideration pros and cons of current PSIM solutions, we came to a general concept to improve the efficiency, flexibility and speed of launch compared to existing platforms. In the process of developing this platform, we take into account all aspects and the specifics of the Israeli market by implementing and realizing the needs of the end user. Great work has been done to analyze and collect information on existing projects and on the basis of this data a highly efficient algorithm has been created that is able to cope with all sorts of incidents, thereby increasing the level of security.
